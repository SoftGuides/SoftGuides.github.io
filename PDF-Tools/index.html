Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best tool for editing PDFs?
Adobe Acrobat is the most powerful desktop PDF editor, while PDFgear and Smallpdf are great for quick editing and conversions. The right tool depends on your needs for security, editing, or online convenience.
How do I compress a PDF file?
Use PDFgear or Smallpdf to reduce file size quickly. The tutorials cover the best compression settings for preserving quality while making PDFs smaller.
Can I sign PDFs online?
Yes. Smallpdf and Adobe Acrobat both support electronic signatures. The tutorials explain how to add signatures, fill forms, and secure signed documents.
